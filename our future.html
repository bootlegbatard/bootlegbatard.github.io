Bootleg Bâtard Bakery is the answer to the near north’s demand for authentic, delicious, artisan breads and pastries. Beginning as a micro bakery, Bootleg Bâtard uses a wood-fired oven and sells their hand-crafted wares at local farmers markets.

We are currently looking for a brick and mortar location.

If you are interested in helping Bootleg Bâtard become a year round brick-and-mortar location, please contact Melina at melina@bootlegbatard.com.
