Crusty breads, flakey pastries, craveable cookies and much more are available from our full-line artisan bakery. Bootleg Bâtard prides itself on choosing the highest quality ingredients, featuring seasonal and local produce when possible. We use time-honored techniques to draw out the very best flavors within each ingredient. Gentle mixing and long fermentation give signature flavors to our hearth-baked breads. Thoughtful preparation of the freshest ingredients defines our savory tarts and sandwiches. Craveable pairings will keep you coming back for our desserts and pastries.

Bootleg Bâtard would love to make a breakfast platter for your morning meeting or a dessert tray for your dinner party. We will tailor a menu to suit your palate. Just email us at info@bootlegbatard.com!
